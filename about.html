About Ralph Lawal Group

A growing multi-division company built on the belief that strategy, creativity, and genuine partnership create lasting value.

Our Origin

Built to Deliver, Designed to Endure

Ralph Lawal Group was founded with a simple but powerful conviction: that great work happens at the intersection of creative thinking, strategic clarity, and committed partnership. What began as a focused pursuit of excellence in branding and digital work has evolved into a multi-division practice serving clients across industries and geographies.

We are headquartered in Dublin, Ireland — a city that sits at the crossroads of European commerce and global ambition — with deep ties and active collaborations across West Africa, particularly in Nigeria. This dual geography gives us both a European sensibility and a grounded understanding of emerging markets.

We are selective about who we work with. We partner with brands and organizations that care about their craft, their audience, and their long-term impact. If that's you, we'd love to talk.

Our Mission

To empower brands, creators, and organizations with the strategy, tools, and creative work they need to grow with confidence.

Our Vision

To be a globally respected group known for the quality of its thinking, the craft of its work, and the depth of its partnerships.

The Founder

Ralph Lawal

Ralph Lawal is a product designer, frontend developer, and brand strategist with roots spanning Dublin and Lagos. He brings a multidisciplinary approach to every challenge — blending visual thinking with technical execution and commercial strategy.

Before founding Ralph Lawal Group, Ralph spent years working across digital marketing, web development, product design, and the creative industries. He also teaches coding through CODING4KIDSHUB, believing strongly that access to technical education can transform lives.

Ralph founded the Group to create a structure where talented people could collaborate at a high level — and where clients could access premium, coherent support across multiple disciplines without the fragmentation of working with many separate vendors.
